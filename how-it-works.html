The Process

From photo to estimate in 60 seconds.

RepairSync's AI pipeline turns damage photos, audio, or text into a verified, unbiased repair cost range — trusted by drivers, shops, and insurers alike.

Roadmap

01
Upload or Describe Damage

Drivers upload damage photos, record audio, or type a description. All three paths feed into the same AI analysis pipeline — no special equipment needed.

02
AI Vision Analysis

Claude Vision identifies damaged parts, severity levels, and repair types — returning structured data in milliseconds. Dents, scratches, cracks, and replacements all detected.

03
Parts & Labor Lookup

Our system queries OEM parts databases and regional labor rate tables, cross-referencing historical invoice data to compute accurate cost ranges by ZIP code.

04
Verified Cost Range

A min-max cost range is calculated per damaged component, with a plain-English summary — "Front bumper, left fender, paint blend needed → $1,580–$1,760."

05
PDF Report & Settlement

Download a branded PDF report with full itemized breakdown — use it to negotiate, settle, or dispute the claim with confidence and verifiable data.
